About Us

LINK LIFE is a non-profit platform dedicated to bridging the gap between blood donors and those in need. Our mission is to ensure that no life is lost due to blood shortage by maintaining a well-connected database of donors.

Our Mission

  • Build a seamless network of voluntary blood donors.
  • Provide a quick and efficient donor search system.
  • Encourage and spread awareness about blood donation.
